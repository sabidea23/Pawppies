Contact Us

Welcome to our "Contact Us" page! We are here to assist you with any inquiries, support needs, or feedback you might have. Your questions and comments are invaluable to us, and we are committed to providing you with prompt and effective responses.

Whether you need more information about our services, require assistance with our website, or wish to share your experiences and suggestions, this is the place to reach out. We are dedicated to ensuring your experience with us is positive and fulfilling.

Please use the form below to send us your messages. Our team is eager to hear from you and will respond as swiftly as possible. Your insights help us to continually improve and serve you better. Thank you for choosing to contact us – we look forward to assisting you!

ARE YOU TRYING TO FIND A HOME FOR YOUR PET?

Congratulations on your efforts to help this animal. First, please read Tips on Finding a Home for a Pet. This library article will guide you through the steps to finding a suitable home. You may want to look through our list of adoption groups to find those in your area. Contact them directly to find out what assistance, if any, they may be able to offer you.

DID YOU INQUIRE ABOUT A PET LISTING, BUT DIDN'T RECEIVE A RESPONSE?

We know it can be frustrating when you don't get a response from a shelter or rescue group. Many of the pet listings on Pawppies are maintained by volunteers. They may only be able to review and respond to requests a few times per week, sometimes less. Many of our groups also receive several applications and inquiries for each pet they have available, which causes delays as it can take time to review each application or inquiry. We’re certain they do want to find the best possible homes for their pets.

We hope this negative experience will not affect your decision to adopt a pet in need. We urge you to continue your search for other pets who match your criteria. Thank you for taking the time to contact us and for thinking adoption first.

DO YOU HAVE CONCERNS ABOUT THE HEALTH OR SAFETY OF A LISTED PET?

Pawppies is a website for nearly 13,000 independent animal shelters and rescue groups to post their adoptable pets. If you are concerned about the welfare of any animal, you may want to consider contacting your local authorities for assistance.

HOW DO I REGISTER MY ANIMAL CENTER ON THE PLATFORM

To register your animal center, sign up as a supplier, fill in the necessary information about your center, and once verified, you can start adding your animals to the platform.
