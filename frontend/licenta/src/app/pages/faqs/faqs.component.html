FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ page! Here you can find answers to some of the most common questions about our pet adoption platform. Whether you're looking to adopt a pet or you're an animal center looking to join our community, we've got you covered.

How do I adopt a pet from the site?

Register as a user, complete the adoption quiz to find your best match, and follow the steps to initiate the adoption process.

What criteria can I use to filter pets?

You can filter pets by age, size, gender, type (dog/cat), breed, health status, and other special needs or preferences.

How do I register my animal center on the platform?

To register your animal center, sign up as a supplier, fill in the necessary information about your center, and once verified, you can start adding your animals to the platform.

Can I update the information about my animal center or pets?

Yes, you can update the information about your animal center and the pets you have listed at any time through your dashboard.

Is there a fee to use the site for adoption or listing animals?

There is no fee to use the site. Our platform is completely free for both adopters and animal centers to encourage the adoption process and help animals find their forever homes.

How can I trust the information provided about the pets?

All information about the pets and animal centers is verified by our team to ensure accuracy and reliability. We work closely with each center to maintain updated and comprehensive pet profiles.

What should I do if I'm interested in a pet?

If you're interested in adopting a pet, you can start the adoption process by clicking the 'Start Adoption Process' button on the pet's profile page. This will guide you through the necessary steps to connect with the animal center.

Can I interact with a pet before deciding to adopt?

Yes, we encourage potential adopters to interact with pets. You can contact the respective animal center to arrange a visit or inquire about the possibility of a virtual interaction.

What happens if the adoption doesn't work out?

We aim for successful adoptions, but if there are unforeseen circumstances, we advise contacting the animal center to discuss the best course of action. It's important to ensure a good match for both the pet and the adopter.

How do you protect my personal data in accordance with GDPR?

We take your privacy and data protection seriously. In compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we implement stringent security measures to safeguard your personal information. We collect only the necessary data, store it securely, and never share it without your explicit consent. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. For more detailed information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

How often is Pawppies updated with new pets?

Pawppies is continuously updated as animal centers post new pets and update their profiles. We recommend checking back frequently or setting up notifications for new updates that match your preferences.

Can I save my search to be notified when new pets are available?

Yes, Pawppies offers a feature to save your search criteria. You can receive email notifications when pets matching your preferences become available, ensuring you don't miss out on any potential matches.

Is the pet I see on Pawppies still available for adoption?

Each animal center is responsible for maintaining the status of their pets. To inquire about a specific pet's availability, please contact the center directly through the information provided on the pet's profile.

How do I meet a pet I am interested in on Pawppies?

Once you find a pet you're interested in, visit the pet's profile page and use the contact details to inquire about meeting the pet. Each center may have different procedures for meetings and introductions.

How is my personal data protected on Pawppies?

Your privacy is crucial to us. Pawppies adheres to GDPR guidelines to ensure your personal data is securely protected. We collect only necessary information and provide you with control over your data, including rights to access, modify, or delete as per GDPR standards.

If you have more questions or need further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us
